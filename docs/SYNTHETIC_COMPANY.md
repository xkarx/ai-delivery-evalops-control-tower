# Synthetic Company

## Minimum dataset

- Company overview
- Product strategy
- 3 quarterly goals
- 5 personas
- 50 persistent customers
- 8 interviews
- 30 support tickets
- 20 feature requests
- 15 bugs
- 8 Slack threads
- Roadmap and 25 backlog items
- 5 decisions
- 5 releases
- 3 incidents
- Analytics event schema and baseline funnel
- 3 experiments
- At least 30 eval cases

## Formats

- Markdown for narrative documents
- JSON for entities
- CSV for analytics
- JSONL for eval datasets

## Rules

- Deterministic seed
- Valid linked IDs
- Realistic timestamps
- Noise, ambiguity, and conflicting evidence
- Data dictionary and scenario manifest
- Clear synthetic-data labels

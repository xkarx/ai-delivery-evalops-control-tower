# DailyCart product strategy

> **Synthetic demo data.** Seed `20260710`; scenario `checkout-friction`. No people, accounts, or events are real.

## Strategic intent

Help customers move from need to a confident purchase with minimal avoidable work, trustworthy expectations, and inclusive interaction.

## Principles

1. Prefer recoverable journeys over fragile speed optimizations.
2. Make price, delivery, and system state legible before commitment.
3. Treat accessibility as task completion, not a checklist afterthought.
4. Require converging qualitative and behavioral evidence before broad investment.
5. Preserve human release decisions when customer or operational risk is material.
